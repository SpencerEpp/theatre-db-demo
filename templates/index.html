Welcome to Horizon

We are a passionate theatre company dedicated to creating engaging, inspiring, and thought-provoking performances for our community. Whether you're here to enjoy a night out, get involved behind the scenes, or support us as a patron, we're thrilled to have you.

About Us

The Horizon Theatre Company has been a cornerstone of local performing arts for over 20 years. Our mission is to empower artists, connect audiences, and enrich lives through live storytelling and shared experience. We produce a wide variety of shows every year — from beloved classics to original works — and welcome everyone to join in the magic.
